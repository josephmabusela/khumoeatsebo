About Us

We are a black owned agricultural business that commercialises developing farmiers. We are also turnaround specialists that develop and implement turnaround strategies in the agricultural and manufacturing sector KET has been in existence since 2004 and boasts vast experience in business turnarounds, development, implemention and enterprise development Last season we have planted over 3 000 hectares of grains in 5 provinces, namely:
Gauteng, Mpumalanga, Free State, North West and Limpopo. This year we are adding Kwa-Zulu Natal and Eastern Cape.

our Services

KET is an aggregator for Tiger Brands and just last season (2021-2022) has aggegated over 30 farmers in 5 provinces planting maize, sugar beans and small white beans. For the first time KET has also supplied Mjesty Oil Mills with soybean and plans to grow this relationship to include sunflower in the next season
